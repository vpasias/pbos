# Bond case
Hypervisor Hosts Interfaces:
- eth0 - service (PXE-admin)
- eth1 - management network
- bond1 (eth2 + eth3) - provider - overlay - storage networks

interfaces & VLANs allocation:
- eth0: service network: 192.168.20.0/24
- eth1: management (API-keepalived) network: 192.168.21.0/24
- bond1.100 (VLAN 100): provider 1 (main) network: 192.168.22.0/24
- bond1.200 (VLAN 200): overlay network: 192.168.23.0/24
- bond1.300 (VLAN 300): storage & rados GW network: 192.168.24.0/24
- bond1.400 (VLAN 400): provider 2 network: 192.168.25.0/24
