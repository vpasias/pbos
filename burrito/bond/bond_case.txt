# Bond case
Hypervisor Hosts Interfaces:
- eth0 - service (PXE-admin)
- bond1 (eth1 + eth2) - cluster (management-overlay-provider)
- bond2 (eth3 + eth4) - storage - rados GW

interfaces & VLANs allocation:
- eth0: service network: 192.168.20.0/24
- bond1 (VLAN 0 - native): management (API-keepalived) network: 192.168.21.0/24
- bond2.100 (VLAN 100): provider 1 (main) network: 192.168.22.0/24
- bond2.200 (VLAN 200): overlay network: 192.168.23.0/24
- bond2.300 (VLAN 300): storage & rados GW network: 192.168.24.0/24
- bond2.400 (VLAN 400): provider 2 network: 192.168.25.0/24
